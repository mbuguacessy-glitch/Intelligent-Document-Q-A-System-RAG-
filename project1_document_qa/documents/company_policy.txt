COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT — Acme Automation Agency

REFUND POLICY
Clients may request a full refund within 14 days of project commencement
if no deliverables have been shared. After 14 days, refunds are issued
on a pro-rata basis depending on work completed. Refund requests must
be submitted in writing to accounts@acme.co.ke.

WORKING HOURS
Our team operates Monday to Friday 8am to 6pm East Africa Time.
Emergency support is available on weekends for Enterprise clients only.
Response time for standard support tickets is 24 hours.

PAYMENT TERMS
All invoices are due within 30 days of issue. Late payments incur a
2% monthly interest fee. Payments must be made in Kenyan Shillings
unless otherwise agreed in the project contract.

CONFIDENTIALITY
All client data, documents, and business information shared with Acme
Automation Agency is treated as strictly confidential. We do not share
client information with third parties without written consent.

PROJECT DELIVERY
Standard projects are delivered within the agreed timeline stated in
the project proposal. Delays caused by client feedback cycles are not
counted against the delivery timeline. All deliverables are shared via
a secure client portal.
